  2. Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 20, 2026

Bhagwati Mandap Bazaar ("we", "us", "our") is dedicated to protecting the privacy of our clients, decorators, partners, and visitors. This Privacy Policy details how we handle, secure, and utilize data collected through our website and business interactions.

1. Information Collection

We gather personal information that you provide voluntarily when contacting us or ordering event products. This include:

  • Identity details: Name, business association name, designation.
  • Contact details: Telephone numbers, email addresses, delivery/billing addresses.
  • Inquiry parameters: Specific event setups, mandap measurements, product selection details, and text notes.

2. Processing & Usage of Information

Your business parameters and details are utilized for the following core operations:

  • Generating price quotes and sending digital product catalogs.
  • Processing fabrication orders, arranging freight, and dispatching invoices.
  • Addressing support questions regarding setups and maintenance instructions.
  • Sharing business notifications or updates regarding new catalogs (only if opted in).

3. Data Security & Storage

We execute appropriate technological and operational safeguards designed to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your information. Access to data is restricted strictly to employees needing the information to fulfill business services (e.g. billing and manufacturing teams).

4. Cookies and Web Analytics

Our website implements standard functional cookies to facilitate navigation and log visits. These cookies contain no sensitive details. You have the option to disable cookies directly in browser parameters, though this may limit navigation ease on some elements.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not trade, sell, or rent your identity data to third-party advertisers. Information is only shared with authorized logistics and transportation partners for the sole purpose of delivery dispatch, or as demanded by legal authorities in compliance with Indian jurisdiction.

6. Section 43A IT Act Compliance

In compliance with Section 43A of the Indian Information Technology Act, 2000, we apply reasonable security methods to safeguard your sensitive business data. For clarifications or corrections, you can reach our Grievance Desk.
