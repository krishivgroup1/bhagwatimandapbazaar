Our Identity

Shaping Memorable Celebrations

Bhagwati Mandap Bazaar has grown from a local workshop into one of Western India's most respected names in event equipment and wedding mandap manufacturing.

Established in Rajkot, Gujarat, we have spent years refining the art of mandap fabrication. We merge traditional Indian wedding motifs with state-of-the-art metal engineering to create structures that are not only visually stunning but exceptionally sturdy and safe.

Our customer base ranges from local decorators in Rajkot to luxury destination wedding organizers across the nation. Every product leaving our factory carries a promise of premium aesthetic detailing and structural integrity.

Our Vision

To be the absolute standard of excellence in the event manufacturing industry. We envision equipping every wedding planner with innovative, durable, and highly ornate decoration solutions, keeping Indian heritage alive while adopting contemporary manufacturing designs.

Our Mission

To design and construct safe, premium-quality wedding mandaps, stages, and lighting gear that empower designers to push the boundaries of style. We achieve this by offering competitive wholesale factory pricing, stellar support, and unmatched customization parameters.

Our Principles

Core Business Values

These foundational values guide our decisions, our production standards, and how we interact with our customers and partners every single day.

Uncompromising Safety

We prioritize structural integrity in all heavy-duty mandaps and stages to guarantee absolute safety during crowded events.

Exquisite Craftsmanship

Our metal welders and artists detail every curve, gold paint coat, and fabric trim to deliver products of absolute luxury.

Value Pricing

By producing completely in-house, we pass substantial cost savings directly onto our clients, assuring higher margins for them.

Reliability & Trust

We value the event timelines of our clients. When we commit to a supply date, we ensure the products arrive on time.

Our Journey

Milestones & History

A timeline detailing how we have consistently expanded our horizons to better serve the event decoration community.

2011

The Beginning

Bhagwati Mandap Bazaar opened its doors in Rajkot as a boutique fabrication shop specializing in basic wooden and simple iron mandaps for local decorators.

2016

Facility Expansion

To meet growing demand, we relocated to Navagam Main Road, investing in heavy welding machinery and establishing a dedicated showroom displaying complete mock wedding setups.

2020

Nationwide Distribution

We initiated logistics pathways to supply our premium event pillars, backdrops, and lighting arrays to wedding decorators across other states, including Rajasthan, Maharashtra, and Madhya Pradesh.

2026

Modern Digital Horizon

Launching custom catalogs, adopting structural alloys for lighter weight and increased stability, and building a digitized customer assistance flow for global clients.
